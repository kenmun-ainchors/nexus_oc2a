## L-028 — Notion Incident (11:34–11:45 AEST)

**What happened:**
- We ran 22+ rapid Notion API writes (bulk ticket sync, new pages, status updates)
- Notion backlog view showed "Something went wrong" — all views broken
- Spent ~10 min diagnosing, found `{}` empty-object bug in `notion_update_ticket`, patched and re-cleaned 30 pages
- Ken logged out/back in → ALL Notion table pages broken (not just ours)
- **Root cause: Notion platform incident, not our writes**

**The `{}` bug was still real (CHG-0290 worth keeping):**
- `notion_update_ticket` was sending `{Sprint: {}, "Planned Date": {}, "Delivered Date": {}}` on empty fields
- Invalid Notion property value — always omit or use null, never `{}`
- Fix applied regardless — it was a latent bug that would eventually cause real corruption

**The learning:**
1. Before diagnosing "did our writes break Notion" — check Notion status page first: https://www.notionstatuspage.com
2. A platform incident will look identical to a self-inflicted DB corruption
3. Coincidental timing ≠ causation — verify scope (is it just our DB or all of Notion?) before diving deep
4. Diagnostic signal: if ALL table pages are broken (not just ours) → platform issue, not us

**Action going forward:**
- Add Notion status check to health-check.sh (optional, low priority)
- The `{}` fix and rate-limit delay are both valid improvements regardless

## 3. Name Construction & Etymology

### Word Breakdown

| Component | Origin | Meaning |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Aev-** | Latin *aevum* | The eternal dimension — the mode of existence between mortal finite time and divine infinite time. Neither past nor future. The timeless layer that underlies everything. The theological concept used by Aquinas to describe angels and celestial bodies — beings that exist *in* time but are not *bound by* it. |
| **-lith** | Greek *lithos* | Stone — permanence, bedrock, foundation, structural immovability. Used in technology contexts (monolith, Novalith, ElectraLith) to signal foundational infrastructure. |

### Synthesis

**Aevlith = The timeless foundation.** The bedrock that exists outside of any single technology cycle. Not a trend. Not a moment. The infrastructure that was built to outlast everything built on top of it.

This is precisely what Nexus *is* inside AInchors: the invisible platform layer that will still be running when the consulting methodologies, training courses, and client engagements have all evolved. The foundation endures.

## 4. Star Wars Thematic Alignment

AInchors' naming convention is locked to Star Wars (confirmed by Ken and Angie, 2026-05-03). Every platform module and agent carries a Star Wars name. Aevlith sits within this cosmology without being a direct Star Wars reference — it is a **companion word** that carries the same energy.

### The Force Connection

The Cosmic Force in Star Wars is described as:
- An energy field that exists outside of time — it sees past, present, and future simultaneously
- The deep, unifying current beneath the Living Force — the structural layer that binds all things
- The "eternal dimension" that Jedi Masters like Qui-Gon Jinn and Yoda connect with at the deepest level of meditation

*Aevum* (the Latin root) is the closest real-world philosophical concept to the Cosmic Force's relationship with time. It is the dimension of things that are created but do not age — permanent, underlying, ever-present.

**Aevlith is the stone of the Cosmic Force. The eternal foundation beneath Nexus.**

5. **Globally pronounceable** — clean in English, Bahasa, Arabic, Mandarin (critical for AU + MY + GCC expansion)
6. **Premium without trying** — invented, ownable, no pop culture baggage

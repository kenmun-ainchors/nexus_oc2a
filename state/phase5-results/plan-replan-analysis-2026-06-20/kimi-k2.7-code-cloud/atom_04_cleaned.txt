1. Additive or replacement
- Additive. The Friday “week in review” post is a new weekly slot layered o
on top of the existing Tue/Wed/Thu crons. The 12-post / 4-movement sequence
sequence remains unchanged.

2. Scheduling options

Option A: Standalone Friday recap
- Add a full, original Friday post that reflects on the week’s movement/the
movement/theme.
- Trade-offs: Stronger weekly narrative and extra engagement opportunity; r
requires more content creation and may increase posting frequency fatigue.

Option B: Lightweight Friday digest
- Add a short Friday post that links back to and summarizes the three Tue/W
Tue/Wed/Thu posts.
- Trade-offs: Low incremental creative effort and reinforces prior content;
content; can feel repetitive if it mirrors Thursday’s post too closely, and
and may earn lower standalone engagement.

3. Guard against double-posting
- Maintain a single content calendar with a unique constraint on (channel, 
publish_date). The Friday cron checks for any existing LinkedIn post schedu
scheduled that Friday before publishing; if one exists, the job aborts and 
logs a warning instead of posting.

4. Recommended cron expression for Friday 07:30 AEST
- `30 7 * * 5`
- Configure the job timezone to AEST, e.g. `TZ=Australia/Sydney` (or `TZ=Au
`TZ=Australia/Brisbane` for fixed AEST without DST).

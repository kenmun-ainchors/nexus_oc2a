Fondy Foong

  • HRDCorp Certified Trainer

  • Professional Life Coach

Education:

B.IT Multimedia Technology Management, Multimedia University

Fondy Foong is a passionate and engaging HRDCorp Certified Trainer with over 20 years of diverse corporate and business leadership experience. With a strong foundation in technology, operations, business development, and human capital growth, she brings real-world practicality into every training room.

Today, as a trainer, professional life and career coach, business consultant, Fondy believes that transformation begins with awareness. Her signature approach integrates self-discovery tools, structured reflection, and actionable frameworks that empower participants to take ownership of their growth. Known for being lively, energetic, highly engaging, and participant-focused, Fondy creates safe yet challenging learning environments where individuals are inspired to think deeper, act boldly, and grow intentionally.

Fondy Foong

  • HRDCorp Certified Trainer

  • Professional Life Coach

  • B.IT Multimedia Technology Management, Multimedia University

Fondy Foong is a passionate and engaging HRDCorp Certified Trainer with over 20 years of diverse corporate and business leadership experience. With a strong foundation in technology, operations, business development, and human capital growth, she brings real-world practicality into every training room.

Today, as a trainer, professional life and career coach, business consultant, Fondy believes that transformation begins with awareness.
